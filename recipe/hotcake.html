Hotcake Recipe

We are going to do a classic American Breakfast Hotcakes.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups self-raising Flour
  • 1 teaspoon Baking Power
  • 1/4 cup Caster Sugar
  • 2 Eggs
  • 60g Butter
  • 1 cup Milk (250ml)
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla

Instructions

  1. Sift the flour and baking powder into a medium mixing bowl. Stir in the sugar. Combine the eggs, butter, milk and vanilla in a jug. Make a well in the centre of the dry ingredients. Pour in the milk mixture and use a whisk to mix to a smooth batter. Cover with plastic wrap. Stand 10 minutes.

  2. Heat a medium non-stick frypan over medium to low heat. Lightly oil with cooking spray. Pour 1/4 cup of the batter into the pan and cook for 2 minutes or until small bubbles appear on the surface. Turn over and cook for 1 minute or until golden and cooked through. Remove from pan and keep warm. Repeat with remaining mixture.

  3. Serve hotcakes with extra butter and maple syrup.
